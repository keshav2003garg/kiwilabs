What is Tendinopathy?

Understanding Tendinopathy

You've probably heard of tendinitis—but did you know that's an outdated term? Tendinopathy is the real issue, and it's not just inflammation. It requires proper loading and rehab strategies, not just rest and medication.

Why Traditional Rehab Fails

Most rehab plans focus on rest, anti-inflammatory treatments, and light band exercises that don't truly address the problem. This leads to a frustrating cycle of pain and setbacks.
