Why I Became a Coach

From Electrician to Fitness Coach

My journey wasn't a straight path. As an electrician, I struggled with anxiety and depression while trying to maintain my physical health. It was during these challenging times that I discovered the transformative power of fitness.

Finding My Purpose

"Fitness saved me, and now I help others take control of their health—physically and mentally. I understand the struggles because I've been there, and I'm here to guide you through your journey with compassion and understanding."

A Message of Hope

To anyone struggling with mental health while trying to get fit: You're not alone. Your journey is valid, and it's okay to take it one step at a time. Together, we can build a sustainable approach to health that works for you.
